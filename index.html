Support & Documentation

About VerseMarking

VerseMarking is a precision audio utility designed to automatically detect and mark silences—such as verses, chapters, or cue points—within spoken-word audio files. Built entirely in your browser, it guarantees complete privacy. Your audio is never uploaded to an external server; everything is processed safely and locally on your machine.

How it Works

Our detection engine rapidly scans the audio waveform's amplitude against an internal volume threshold. When the audio drops below this threshold for the uninterrupted duration specified in your Min Silence (sec) setting, the system flags it as a break and drops a precision marker directly in the center of that gap.

Important Instructions

  • Verse Gaps: Maintain exactly 1 second gap between each verse.
  • No Gaps: Do not add any 1-second gap within a verse, as the application will not function correctly.
  • Tail Trim: Trim the tail in Logic Pro so the total gap remains precisely 2 seconds.

Common Issues

  • Audio file won't load or process: Make sure your file is a standard uncompressed PCM .wav file.
  • Incorrect Verse Marking: The system currently only supports audio files with a 48kHz (48000Hz) sample rate. Ensure your DAW export settings match this requirement.
